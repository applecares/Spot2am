spot2am

Move a playlist, album, or track between Spotify and Apple Music. No login to read.

    Apple Music off

    Optional. Better matching and one-click Add. Otherwise use the CSV with TuneMyMusic.

    1. Sign in at music.apple.com.
    2. DevTools → Network → click a song.
    3. Open a request to amp-api.music.apple.com → Headers.
    4. Copy authorization: Bearer … → Developer token.
    5. Copy media-user-token → Media-user token.

    Needed to match and create the Spotify playlist. The token expires about hourly — grab it just before transferring. Otherwise use the CSV with TuneMyMusic.

    1. Sign in at open.spotify.com.
    2. DevTools → Network → play a song.
    3. Open a request to api.spotify.com → Headers.
    4. Copy authorization: Bearer … → Spotify token.
    Runs on your machine. CSV saved every run.
